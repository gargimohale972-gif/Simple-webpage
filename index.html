Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2â€“3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Beat eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan: Heat pan and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook: Pour eggs and tilt pan evenly.
  4. Add fillings: Add fillings to one side.
  5. Fold: Fold omelette and cook 1 more minute.
  6. Serve: Slide onto plate and enjoy.
